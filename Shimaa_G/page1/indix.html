second recipe

The way the Lebanese kibbeh is made

Ingredients

  1. A kilogram of minced meat
  2. Half a kilogram of fine bulgur
  3. Half a cup of chopped walnuts
  4. Two tablespoons of pepper molasses
  5. Half a teaspoon of fine red pepper
  6. A quarter of a teaspoon of salt

How to prepare

Wash the bulgur well, leave it in a strainer until the water drains from it,
then add the minced meat to it

Add pepper, salt, walnuts and capsicum molasses, to the meat and bulgur mixture

Mix the mixture well, and knead the ingredients until you get a soft and cohesive dough

The dough is spread out on a serving plate, and it can also be formed in the form
of longitudinal discs or in the form of circular balls, as desired.

The kibbeh is decorated with whole walnuts or pomegranate granules,
and olive oil is added to it.

How tabbouleh works

Preparation time 10 minutes
Difficulty level easy>
The number of servings is enough for 6 people

Ingredients

2 bunches parsley, finely chopped

Five tomatoes, cut into small pieces.

Onion, medium, finely chopped.

Quarter cup of bulgur, fine.

Three tablespoons of lemon juice.

Two tablespoons of pomegranate molasses

Two tablespoons of olive oil.

¼ teaspoon of black pepper.

¼ teaspoon red chili powder

Quarter of a teaspoon of salt.

How to prepare

Wash a quarter cup of bulgur well, drain, and soak for 10 minutes

Wash the parsley, drain well from the water, then chop finely

Cut the vegetables and prepare, as the onions are mixed with
the tomatoes and bulgur in a wide and deep dish.

Add chopped parsley, season with spices, lemon juice, pomegranate molasses,
black pepper, red pepper powder, salt and olive oil.

Mix the ingredients well, and the tabbouleh is ready to be served
